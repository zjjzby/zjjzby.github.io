I am an AI developer technology engineer at [NVIDIA](https://www.nvidia.com/). I received my Ph.D. in Computer Engineering from the [University of Southern California](https://www.usc.edu/), where I was advised by Professor [Viktor K. Prasanna](https://sites.usc.edu/prasanna/) in the [FPGA/Parallel Computing Lab](https://fpga.usc.edu/). My research interests span high-performance computing, compiler design, computer architecture, reconfigurable computing, and graph machine learning.

Prior to USC, I earned my M.S. from the State Key Laboratory of ASIC and System at [Fudan University](https://www.fudan.edu.cn/en/), under the guidance of Professor [Jun Han](https://sme.fudan.edu.cn/5f/da/c31145a352218/page.htm) and Professor [Xiaoyang Zeng](https://sme.fudan.edu.cn/60/76/c31158a352374/page.htm).

**Ph.D. Thesis:** [Hardware-Software Codesign for Accelerating Graph Neural Networks on FPGA](https://www.proquest.com/openview/f3117d4ea8c40a83331ee7d4c12ddbad/1?pq-origsite=gscholar&cbl=18750&diss=y)

**Professional Service:** I have served as a reviewer over 80 times for journals and conferences, including:

- **Journals** (70+): IEEE TVLSI, IEEE TIP, IET Computer Vision, IET Image Processing, Neurocomputing, Engineering Applications of Artificial Intelligence, Information Sciences, and others.
- **Conferences** (10+): ASICON 2021, ICSICT 2022, IEEE International Radar Conference 2023.
