Bingyi Zhang

AI Developer Technology Engineer, NVIDIA

I am an AI developer technology engineer at NVIDIA. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Engineering from the University of Southern California, where I was advised by Professor Viktor K. Prasanna in the FPGA/Parallel Computing Lab. My research interests span high-performance computing, compiler design, computer architecture, reconfigurable computing, and graph machine learning.

Prior to USC, I earned my M.S. from the State Key Laboratory of ASIC and System at Fudan University, under the guidance of Professor Jun Han and Professor Xiaoyang Zeng.

Ph.D. Thesis: Hardware-Software Codesign for Accelerating Graph Neural Networks on FPGA

Professional Service: I have served as a reviewer over 80 times for journals and conferences, including:

  • Journals (70+): IEEE TVLSI, IEEE TIP, IET Computer Vision, IET Image Processing, Neurocomputing, Engineering Applications of Artificial Intelligence, Information Sciences, and others.
  • Conferences (10+): ASICON 2021, ICSICT 2022, IEEE International Radar Conference 2023.
